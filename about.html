Mission

Save American lives by replacing conventional combat roles.

Goals

  1. Demonstrate a State of the Art Agent for DOOM RL.
  2. Demonstrate the ability to understand decision making process
  3. Generalize the performance of an agent trained with Reinforcement Learning in a First-Person Shooter environment to human level performance in the real world on a stationary platform.
  4. Generalize the performance of an agent to human level performance in the real world on a mobile platform.
